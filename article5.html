Ethics Article 5 – After the midterm demonstration

By Jake Duus | November 2025

The things in my artifacts that are mine are the articles themselves. The background picture on my website is from a random google search. The whitetail deer photo on my CWD artifact is from Wikipedia. The vehicle sign-out sheet was AI assisted. I already had one that we use. I then pretty much communicated with at and had it copy it with some improvements that I wanted in the excel file. Things like the Trinity Bible College logo I wanted in the background. And also the coloring, I wanted that updated for a more professional look. The whole layout of my website was AI assisted as well. I gave the AI 3 websites that I had visited and I really liked the way those were presented and operated. AI gave me three different options of layouts that it could create for my website. I chose the one that had more rustic and premium features. The other photos I used for the background came later.

The about me page was AI assisted as well. I gave chatgpt some information about me, then I prompted it to write it into an about me page for my website. Through the other assignments it is not in my current voice. I played with it and the last edit I gave the ai to use was of a sarcastic nature. I liked the end result to I left it as is. In the future I will work with a trained AI to have it write that in my own voice and proof read it to ensure the authentic feel of how I write.

I will continue to use AI to assist in what I write in the future. I will need to ensure that my own voice profile is used. And review and revise a few times to ensure that all content is accurate and no AI generated sources are used. I will most likely used AI to create outlines of papers and edit them to my liking. That is how I foresee myself using AI in the near, foreseeable future. In the long run I can see myself using it for more complex projects. I am not a professional with AI as of this moment. But I can see how this will be very useful in the future.
