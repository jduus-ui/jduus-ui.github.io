About Me

Hey, I'm Jake Duus. I'm the Fleet and Grounds Manager at Trinity Bible College in Ellendale, North Dakota. Fancy title, I know. Really, it just means if it's got an engine or needs mowing, I'm the guy people call before it catches fire.

I'm also a part-time student at the college. Because apparently, I thought juggling classes, work, and real life sounded like fun. Before this gig, I served in the military, where I picked up a few useful skills like discipline, teamwork, and pretending instant coffee is drinkable.

My faith keeps me grounded. I try to live by this line from Ecclesiastes: "Whatever your hand finds to do, do it with all your might." I take that seriously, whether I'm fixing a stubborn mower, helping a student, or running the grill like it's a mission.

When I'm off the clock, you'll find me tinkering with cars, riding my motorcycle, wandering outside, or traveling with my wife. If the day ends with ribs on the grill, that's what I call a good one.
