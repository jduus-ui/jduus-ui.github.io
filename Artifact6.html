Snow Removal Vehicle Notice

Email Template | Fleet and Grounds Management

This email template was created to speed up notifications to staff and students on campus regarding mandatory vehicle relocation for snow removal operations. The template ensures consistent, clear communication and helps maintain efficient snow removal schedules across campus facilities.

Email Template
Subject: Mandatory Vehicle Relocation for Snow Removal — [Date]

To all staff, faculty, and students,

This is a mandatory notice that all vehicles must be moved from the following locations to allow for scheduled snow removal:

[List specific parking lots / locations here]

Snow removal operations will begin at [Start Time] on [Date] and will continue until approximately [End Time].

Any vehicle that is not moved by the required time may be impacted by snow removal activities.

Please ensure your vehicle is relocated in advance so our team can conduct operations safely and efficiently.

Thank you for your immediate compliance.

[Your Name]
Fleet and Grounds Manager
[College Name]

This standardized template helps ensure that all necessary information is communicated clearly and promptly, reducing confusion and improving compliance with snow removal schedules. Simply fill in the bracketed placeholders with the relevant details for each snow removal event.
