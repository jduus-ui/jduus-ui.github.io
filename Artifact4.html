The Chickadee

By Jacob Duus

The Chickadee. It is my favorite songbird. I am not an expert on the bird by any means. But I did have a random thought about it. "Do they have a winter range and a summer range?" I did some research and found that they were year long residence where they were. If they did move around it was not far at all.
